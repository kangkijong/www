CHARACTERS

MAIN CHARACTERS

James Bond

A former MI6 agent who was known as 007 during his service and has been retired for five years at the start of the film.

Madeleine Swann

A psychotherapist, daughter of Mr. White, and Bond's love interest who assisted him in his mission in the film Spectre.

Lyutsifer Safin

A terrorist leader on a revenge mission against Spectre who later becomes Bond's new adversary by coming into conflict with him and Swann.

Nomi

A new "00" agent who entered active service some time after Bond's retirement and was assigned the 007 number.

OTHER CHARACTERS

Paloma

A CIA agent assisting Bond.

Felix Leiter

Bond's friend and a CIA field officer.

Gareth Mallory / M

Head of MI6 and Bond's superior officer.

Q

MI6's Quartermaster who outfits "00" agents with equipment for use in the field.

Eve Moneypenny

M's secretary and Bond's ally.

Ernst Stavro Blofeld

Bond's arch-enemy and foster brother. He is the founder and head of the criminal syndicate Spectre and is now in MI6 custody.
