ABOUT

THE HISTORY OF CHELSEA FC
With over a century of football played at a prime west London location so fundamental to the birth of Chelsea Football Club in 1905, there is a great story to tell, full of ambition, star players, dramatic quests for trophy success and fashionable style, and at times even a swagger too, and we tell that tale here.
THE HISTORY OF CHELSEA FC WOMEN
A 21st Century success story, Chelsea Women are big news on the domestic and world stages, but a line can be traced back to 100 years earlier with Stamford Bridge hosting female footballers before a nationwide ban. When that was lifted, a Chelsea team were quick out the blocks but it took a relaunch and then assimilation into the main club structure before really starting to move upwards.

TICKETS

How to Buy Tickets

HOME LEAGUE MATCHES
Tickets for home Premier League matches go on sale 42 days before the match. Tickets can be applied for as follows:

Week 1 – one ticket per member
Week 2 – two extra tickets per season ticket holders and members (maximum of three per person).
Week 3 – general sale (four tickets per person)


All match ticket sales are subject to availability.
AWAY LEAGUE MATCHES
Tickets for away Premier League matches go on sale 28 days before the match. Tickets can be applied for as follows:

Week 1 – one ticket per season ticket holder
Week 2 – one ticket per member
Week 3 – two extra tickets per season ticket holders and members


All match ticket sales are subject to availability.
DOMESTIC CUP/EUROPEAN HOME GAMES
Tickets for domestic cup and European home matches go on sale 42 days before the match where possible.
Tickets can be applied for as follows:

Week 1 – one ticket per season ticket holder
Week 2 – one ticket per member
Week 3 – two extra tickets per season ticket holders and members (maximum of three per person).
Week 4 – general sale (four tickets per person)


All match ticket sales are subject to availability.

DOMESTIC CUP/EUROPEAN AWAY GAMES
Tickets for domestic cup and European away matches go on sale 42 days before the match where possible. Tickets can be applied for as follows:

Week 1* – one ticket per season ticket holder
Week 2 – one ticket per member
Week 3** – two extra tickets per season ticket holders and members (maximum of three per person). ** For European away matches, week three will not be applicable as tickets are sold one ticket per season ticket holder/member.


All match ticket sales are subject to availability.
